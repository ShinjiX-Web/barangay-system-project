About the System

Serving the Community Through Better Records

This system was built to modernize the barangay's administrative operations — replacing manual, paper-based processes with a fast, reliable digital platform that helps officials serve residents more effectively.

Barangay staff can manage resident profiles, track document requests, and generate official certificates from a single, easy-to-use dashboard.

System Features

Everything Officials Need in One Place

👤

Resident Management

Maintain complete and up-to-date resident profiles including personal information, household details, and history.

📄

Document Requests

Receive, track, and process certificate and clearance requests efficiently with a clear workflow for staff.

🖨️

Certificate Generation

Automatically generate print-ready official documents using pre-approved barangay templates.

🔐

Secure Role Access

Role-based login ensures only authorized barangay staff can access sensitive resident data and records.

Reports & Statistics

Generate population reports, document statistics, and other insights needed for barangay planning.

Records Archive

Searchable archive of all past records, requests, and issued documents for easy retrieval and audit.
