About the System

Serving the Community Through Better Records

This system was built to modernize the barangay's administrative operations — replacing manual, paper-based processes with a fast, reliable digital platform that helps officials serve residents more effectively.

Barangay staff can manage resident profiles, track document requests, and generate official certificates from a single, easy-to-use dashboard.

Resident Management

Document Requests

Certificate Generation

Secure Role Access
